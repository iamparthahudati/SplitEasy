Design a premium mobile app splash screen for "SplitEasy" on a
390x844px iPhone 14 canvas. Show the screen completely flat and
front-facing — no device angle, no perspective tilt, no 3D effect.
Screen fills the entire frame edge to edge.

BACKGROUND:
A deep, smooth vertical gradient. Top: #1E1B4B (near-black dark indigo).
Middle: #2D2A6E. Bottom: #3730A3. The gradient must feel like the
deepest part of the ocean at night — dark, vast, expensive.
Absolutely no electric blue, no bright cobalt, no neon.
Dark and rich the entire way down. No banding, perfectly smooth.

LOGO MARK (centered, 10% above vertical center):
A perfect circle, 72px diameter. Split exactly down the center
by a razor-thin 1px white vertical line. Left half: solid white fill.
Right half: white stroke outline only, 1.5px, completely transparent
inside. The two halves together form one complete circle.
NO drop shadow on the logo. NO 3D effect. NO glow halo that is
visible as a distinct shape. The logo sits flat within the gradient
as if it belongs there. Behind it: an extremely subtle radial
brightness — not a visible circle, just a very slight lightening
of the gradient directly behind the logo, as if the logo is
part of the background, not on top of it.

WORDMARK (12px below logo, centered):
"SplitEasy" — geometric sans-serif, weight 800, size 34px,
pure white, letter-spacing -1px. The logo and wordmark together
form one single visual unit with minimal gap between them.

DIVIDER (12px below wordmark, centered):
A horizontal line, 36px wide, 1px tall, white at 20% opacity.
Nearly invisible — a breath, not a line.

TAGLINE (10px below divider, centered):
"SPLIT BILLS · KEEP FRIENDS" — all caps, weight 500, size 10px,
white at 40% opacity, letter-spacing 3.5px.
This text should be noticeably dimmer than the wordmark —
it is a whisper, not a statement.

VERSION (40px from bottom, centered):
"v1.0.0" — weight 400, size 10px, white at 20% opacity.

OVERALL FEEL:
Linear.app meets a premium fintech product. The screen should
feel like it was designed by someone who has studied Apple,
Stripe, and Notion. Dark, considered, typographically precise.
Every element feels intentional. The empty space is not empty —
it is breathing room. No element competes with another.
The logo is the only thing that matters. Everything else supports it.

Render as a flat UI screen, perfectly front-facing,
ultra sharp, no device frame, no mockup angle,
no drop shadows on any UI element, no lens flare,
no bokeh, no photography effects. Pure screen design.
